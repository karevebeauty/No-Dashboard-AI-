Complete guide for using your SMS agent to automate sales outreach, LinkedIn prospecting, and lead research.

Your agent can now handle complete sales workflows:
- 🔍 **LinkedIn Prospecting** - Find and research prospects

**Use Responsibly:**
- ✅ Always provide opt-out options
- ✅ Respect LinkedIn rate limits (built-in)
- ✅ Follow CAN-SPAM and GDPR requirements
- ✅ Use for B2B outreach only

- ❌ Don't scrape personal data without permission

- This tool uses conservative rate limits
- Always review messages before sending
- LinkedIn may restrict automated activity
- Use manual verification for important outreach

## Best Practices

### Outreach Do's ✅

1. **Personalize Every Message**
   - Reference specific details
   - Mention recent achievements
   - Show genuine interest

2. **Provide Value First**
   - Share insights
   - Offer resources
   - Help before asking

3. **Keep It Brief**
   - LinkedIn: < 150 words
   - Email: < 200 words
   - Focus on one CTA

4. **Test and Iterate**
   - A/B test subject lines
   - Try different CTAs
   - Adjust based on response rates

5. **Follow Up Strategically**
   - 3-4 touches maximum
   - Space out 3-4 days
   - Add value each time

### Outreach Don'ts ❌

1. **Don't Send Generic Messages**
   ```
   ❌ "I'd love to connect"
   ✅ "Saw your post about ML infrastructure..."
   ```

2. **Don't Ask for Too Much**
   ```
   ❌ "Can we schedule a 1-hour demo?"
   ✅ "Open to a quick 15-min chat?"
   ```

3. **Don't Overwhelm**
   ```
   ❌ 10 messages in 2 weeks
   ✅ 3 messages over 7-10 days
   ```

4. **Don't Ignore Responses**
   - Reply within 24 hours
   - Be helpful and responsive
   - Respect their time

5. **Don't Spam**
   - Respect rate limits
   - Honor opt-outs
   - Follow LinkedIn TOS

- ✅ Include physical address
- ✅ Provide clear opt-out

- ✅ Use accurate subject lines

**GDPR (EU Prospects):**
- ✅ Get consent for contact
- ✅ Explain data usage
- ✅ Allow data deletion requests
- ✅ Maintain privacy policy

**LinkedIn Terms:**
- ✅ Conservative automation
- ✅ Manual review of messages
- ✅ Respect rate limits
- ✅ Don't scrape at scale

### Ethical Outreach

1. **Be Honest**
   - Don't misrepresent your offering
   - Be clear about pricing
   - Set realistic expectations

2. **Be Respectful**
   - Honor opt-outs immediately

   - Share insights freely

## Troubleshooting

### LinkedIn Issues

**"Rate limit exceeded"**
- Wait 24 hours
- Limits reset daily
- Use manual outreach for urgent contacts

**"Profile not found"**
- URL may have changed
- Person may have left company
- Try searching by name + company

**"Connection request failed"**
- May already be connected
- Profile settings may restrict
- Try sending message instead

### Campaign Issues

**"Low response rate"**
- Review message personalization
- Check targeting (ICP match)
- Adjust value proposition
- Test different CTAs

**"Messages not sending"**
- Check campaign status (active?)
- Verify contact data complete
- Review rate limit status
